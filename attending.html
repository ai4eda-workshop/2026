Getting There

  • By car: UCLA is located at 405 Hilgard Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90095. Engineering VI is in the center of campus. Visitor parking is available in several structures on campus.
  • Parking: See UCLA visitor parking information for rates and locations. We recommend Parking Structure 8 (near Engineering VI).
  • By bus: There is a bus stop in front of Engineering VI, with multiple bus lines. Please see UCLA public transit information.

Accommodations

  • The UCLA Luskin Conference Center is located on campus and provides convenient accommodation.
  • Discount code for Luskin: You will see a discount code for Luskin after registration.
  • Several other hotels are available in the Westwood and West LA area near campus.
