Bio

Prof. Yu is an Assistant Professor at the University of Maryland, College Park. His research focuses on novel algorithms, systems, and hardware designs for computing and artificial intelligence. His work has been recognized with a Best Paper Award at DAC (2023), a Best Paper Award at ASPLOS (2025), Best Paper Nominations at DAC, ICCAD, and ASP-DAC, as well as the NVIDIA Academic Research Award and the NSF CAREER Award. He also holds a Visiting Professor position at NVIDIA Research. Prof. Yu earned his Ph.D. from the University of Massachusetts Amherst in 2017.
