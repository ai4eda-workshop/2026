Keynote Speaker: Parthasarathy Ranganathan

Parthasarathy Ranganathan (Google)

Centaurs in the Cloud: A Chipper Future

Bio

Partha Ranganathan is currently a VP, technical Fellow at Google where he is driving next-generation computing systems for the AI-era. He has pioneered numerous impactful innovations touching billions of users, including widely-adopted ideas across all mobile and cloud systems. He has published 100+ papers, is the co-author on the popular "Datacenter as a Computer" textbook, and is a co-inventor on 125+ patents. He has been recognized with numerous awards; notably: top 35 young innovators in the world by MIT Tech Review, top-15 enterprise technology rock star by Business Insider, IEEE & ACM Fellow, and Rice University outstanding young alumni & IIT Madras distinguished alumni awards. Fun fact: Partha is one of few computer scientists to also have an Emmy award!
