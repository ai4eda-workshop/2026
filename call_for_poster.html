Call for Posters

Our workshop welcomes poster submissions on topics related to agent-based approaches for chip design automation, including but not limited to:

  • Agent-based frameworks for performance, power, and area (PPA) prediction;
  • Agent-based design optimization across the EDA pipeline, including HLS, RTL/logic synthesis, and physical design;
  • Agents for code generation, transformation, analysis, and reasoning in chip design workflows;
  • Agents for digital logic (ASICs and FPGAs), analog, mixed-signal, and RF IC designs;
  • Multi-agent system design and collaborative problem solving in chip design;
  • Training or post-training of large foundation models for specialization in chip design;
  • Human-agent interaction and feedback-driven learning for chip design automation;
  • Autonomous test and verification using AI agents;
  • Agent-based approaches for reliability, robustness, aging, and design technology co-optimization (DTCO);
  • Benchmarks, datasets, environments, and evaluation protocols for agents in chip design automation;
  • Other related topics.

Submission Guidelines

  • Formatting: The submission should be a 4-page pdf file following the NeurIPS 2025 template. This submission should contain the title, authors (affiliations and email addresses), a short abstract, background introduction, and a summary of the proposed problem, methods, and main results. References are excluded from the page limit.
  • Originality: The submission is encouraged to be unpublished or recently accepted work due to the forward-looking nature of the workshop.
  • Poster presentation: All accepted submissions will be presented as posters on the workshop day.
  • Submission link: Please submit through the OpenReview submission link.
  • Submission deadline: February 11, 2026, 11:59 PM (AoE)
  • Notification date: February 15, 2026
